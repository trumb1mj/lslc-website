About

Lakeview Speech and Language Clinic provides in-home therapy to children and their families in Oak Park - River Forest, IL and Chicago, IL.

Mission

Our mission is to connect with your child and family to promote and teach skills in order to enrich your lives in all environments. Our therapy sessions are energized and motivational, and use naturalistic materials and activities.

Services

We specialize in providing pediatric speech-language and occupational evaluations and therapies.
